- **Grilled Chicken and Spinach Salad**
- **Why it's 🟢 Green**: This is a high-protein, UPF-free meal, packed with nutrients from spinach and lean protein from grilled chicken. It's also nut-free and low in sugar, aligning perfectly with your dietary goals.
- **Leeds Student Tip**: You can grab pre-cooked chicken breasts from Sainsbury's on the go or grill some fresh chicken from the butcher! Spinach is available at the local market for cheap.
